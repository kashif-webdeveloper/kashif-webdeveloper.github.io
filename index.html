About This Scientific Calculator

Welcome to the ultimate online tool for students, engineers, and developers. Designed by Kashif Dawar, this calculator provides instant access to complex mathematical functions without requiring any software installation.

How to use Scientific Mode: Toggle the tab at the top to access trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan), logarithms (ln, exp), and advanced exponents. Note that trigonometric calculations are performed in Radians for maximum precision in engineering contexts.

Privacy Focused: We believe in data security. All calculations are processed locally on your device using JavaScript. No mathematical data is sent to external servers.
